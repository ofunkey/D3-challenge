Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

Based on the dataset provided, I observed the following trends while analysing the 2014 ACS 1-year estimates. There is a positive correlation between Poverty vs Lack of Healthcare, Poverty vs Smokes and Poverty vs Obesity

There is no significant correlation between Age vs. Lack of Healthcare, Age vs. Smokes and Age vs. Obesity

There is a negative correlation between Household Income vs. Lack of Healthcare, Household Income vs. Smokes and Household Income vs. Obesity.
